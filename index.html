Harsh beginnings

Check it out

Starting out is difficult. Since this was my first experience with any kind of game design, the first year or so was mainly fiddling with the tools available. It's not something to be proud of, but it's definitely good to look back from how far you've come.

My first real project was called 'Being Mario', which featured a third person story teller describing the burden of living as Mario. The idea is manageable, but the execution was lacking, and it never got further than a short demo.

The Senate

Check it out

This project started out as a test for custom graphics. The first few levels were mostly me trying out the possibilities of having different graphics. After designing a few courses, I realized I could weave the levels together and form a big part of world 1.

Almost 2 years later, the results were showing. 72 completely new levels with 23 secret exits spread over them, a playtime of 6-15 hours depending on skill and if you'd find everything.
